Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 9/15/2025

Hi there!

Your privacy matters to us. Here’s how Donezo handles your information:

1. Your Data Stays on Your Device

Everything you enter—tasks, notes, lists—stays on your device. We don’t send your data anywhere, and the app works completely offline.

2. Permissions

We don’t ask for more than we need. The app may ask to access local storage so your tasks can be saved and loaded. That’s it.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

We don’t use ads, analytics, or third-party services. Your data stays private and secure on your device.

4. Security

We do our best to keep your data safe, but please remember that your device’s security is also important.

5. For Families

This app isn’t intended for children under 13, and we don’t knowingly collect their information.

6. Updates to This Policy

If we ever make changes to how we handle your data, we’ll let you know here or in the app.

7. Get in Touch

Questions, concerns, or feedback? Reach out anytime at: shylaxdev@gmail.com
